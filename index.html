What it does

Your production, priced to your contract

Tatanka Ledger turns the shifts you work into the dollars your contract actually owes — and never touches patient data.

No patient data, ever

An encounter is only a number, a time, and CPT codes. No names, no dates of birth, no record numbers — those fields don't exist by design.

Exact compensation math

Bundled CMS wRVU values for every year, with effective-dated contracts that handle fee-schedule changes, raises, and amendments over time.

Paycheck reconciliation

Record what you were actually paid and compare it, period by period, to what your contract owes. Variances are flagged automatically.

Calendar & overlays

See pay, wRVUs, patients, paychecks, and your projected bonus right on the calendar — log today or backfill any day you missed.

Import & export

Bring in your existing spreadsheet, and export CSV reports for any date range to save or email.

Private & yours alone

Cloud backup tied to your own account, readable only by you. No ads, no tracking, and your data is never sold or pooled.

A look inside

Dashboard, calendar, reports, and contract setup.

Privacy first

Built to hold no patient information

Tatanka Ledger is a personal tracking tool, not a medical record. It stores only your own production and compensation data, scoped to your account. You can delete your account and all of its data at any time from within the app.
